Nelson Mandela


  • Nelson Mandela was born on July 18,1918, in Qunu, near to Umatata.

  • He was a South Africa Politician and Philamthropist.

  • His father was Cheif Henry Mandela of the Tembu Tribe.

  • Mandela studied at University College of Fort Hare and the University of Witwatersand and qualified law in 1942.He joined the African National Congress in 1944.

  • Mandela was arrested in 1962 and sentenced to five years imprisonment with hard labour.On june 12.1964, eight of the accused, including Mandela was sentenced to life imprisonment.

  • During his years in prison, Mandela's reputation grew steadily.He was released on February 11,1950.

  • In 1960 Mandela was elected President of the ANC.In 1993 he and President FW de Kierk jointly won the NOBEL PRIZE.

  • Mandela was inaugurated as South Africa's first democratically elected President.

  • After the Presinency, he continued to work with Nelson Mandela Childrens' Fund, Mandela Foundation and Mandela Rhodes Foundation.

  • He is died at his home on 5 December 2013.

  • Nelson Mandela is considered as a revolutionary leader with an ability to motivate other by using his strong words.Mandela is truely a symbol of the good humanity.
